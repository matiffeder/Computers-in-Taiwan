Why Taiwanese Computers Matter?

Taiwan is a place full of creation and cutting-edge technology. The computer products originated from Taiwanese companies have the most recent techniques and fill all over around the world. The famous brands such as ASUS and Acer are all from Taiwan. Even from chips and PCB to monitors and keyboards, most of them are made by Taiwanese companies. Taiwanese companies always have wonderful ideas and always know how to adapt their advanced technology and ideas to their products.

Part of Taiwanese Companies

Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Co.

Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Co. (TSMC) is the biggest foundry in the world. They hold more than 50% market share globally. TSMC was the world's first dedicated semiconductor foundry and has long been the leading company in its field. That means most of the chips in electric products are made by TSMC. After AMD cooperates with TSMC in recent years, the market share of AMD starts to increase and become stronger since the technics that TSMC hold is ahead of Intel and Intel make CPUs themselves.

Taiwan Excellence Award

Taiwan Excellence Award is an Award for the top products from Taiwan including computers and promotes Taiwanese products to the world. The selection mechanism is based on the four major parts that are "R&D", "Design", "Quality" and "Marketing". The awards will be selected by international professional judges to select the "Taiwan Excellence Gold Award" and the "Taiwan Excellence Silver Award", which symbolize the highest honor of Taiwan products. Therefore, if you are looking for the best computer products, try to find the product that shows the Taiwan Excellence Mark.
